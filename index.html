Pranesh Santikellur

I work as an embedded security researcher at Technology Innovation Institute in Abu Dhabi, UAE.
Earlier this year, I have submitted my PhD thesis from IIT Kharagpur on the topic
"Design and analysis of modeling attacks on physically unclonable functions".
I worked for my PhD under the guidance of Professor Rajat Subhra Chakraborty.
I am interested in solving hardware security challenges with machine learning techniques.
